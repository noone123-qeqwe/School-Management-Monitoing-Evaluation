About the Portal

Streamlining document submission between private schools and the Division Office.

Secure Submission

Documents are transmitted securely and made accessible only to authorized personnel.

Real-Time Tracking

Track the status of your submitted documents from submission to acknowledgment and processing.

Multiple File Types

Submit Excel spreadsheets, Word documents, PDF files, and other standard school document formats.

Submission History

Access a complete history of previous submissions with timestamps, document types, and status updates.

School Levels Covered

This portal serves all private school levels under the Division Office.

Kindergarten

Private kindergarten schools can submit enrollment reports, curriculum documents, and compliance requirements.

Elementary

Elementary schools can submit academic reports, faculty credentials, and accreditation documents.

Junior High School

Junior high schools can submit learner profiles, assessment data, and school compliance requirements.

Senior High School

Senior high schools can submit strand offerings, TVL documents, and graduation requirements.
