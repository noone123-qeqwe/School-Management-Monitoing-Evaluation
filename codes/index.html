About the Portal

Streamlining document submission between private schools and the Division Office

Secure Submission

Documents are transmitted securely, accessible only to authorized personnel.

Real-Time Tracking

Track the status of your submitted documents in real time — from submission to acknowledgment and processing.

Multiple File Types

Submit Excel spreadsheets, Word documents, and PDF files. All standard school document formats are supported.

Submission History

Access a complete history of all your past submissions with timestamps, document types, and current status.

School Levels Covered

This portal serves all private school levels under the Division Office

Kindergarten

Private kindergarten schools can submit enrollment reports, curriculum documents, and compliance requirements.

Elementary

Elementary schools can submit academic reports, faculty credentials, and accreditation documents.

Junior High School

Junior high schools can submit learner profiles, assessment data, and assessment requirements.

Senior High School

Senior high schools can submit strand offerings, TVL documents, and graduation requirements.
